Why do I support this project?

Amid the difficult conditions and ongoing war, thousands of displaced families in Gaza are suffering from severe food and water shortages. Our joint campaign aims to provide food baskets for thousands of displaced people in Gaza, both north and south, to alleviate their suffering and provide food support to families living in catastrophic humanitarian conditions.

We invite you to join our relief campaign. Your donation could mean the difference between life and death. Donate now and be part of this noble humanitarian effort.
