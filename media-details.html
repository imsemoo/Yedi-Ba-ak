Hot Meal Donation to Gaza: Daily Meal Distribution for 2000 People

08 July 2025 • 6 min read

The people in Gaza are struggling to survive under great difficulties due to the occupation and blockades. Hot meal distribution is of vital importance for the people of Gaza, who have limited access to their basic needs. In order to meet the daily food needs of the people of Gaza and improve their living conditions, we launched the Hot Meal Donation to Gaza campaign. As part of this campaign, we distribute hot meals to 2,000 people every day.

Lend a helping hand in these difficult days!

Your helping hand is needed to alleviate the emergency situation in Gaza and support these families. With Yedi Başak Humanitarian Aid Association, you can bring hope to those families with the Emergency Aid to Gaza campaign we have launched. Every donation will help us provide a hot meal for families who are left unprotected during these hard days. With 75% of Gaza in ruins, people are now looking for a roof over their heads, and feeling a helping hand.

How can we donate emergency aid to Gaza?

Gaza is becoming increasingly difficult as a region where thousands of families are homeless, struggling with difficult living conditions. The chaos, hunger and inadequate fresh bread show how dire the situation is. With comprehensive sets of burners, stoves and cooking pots, our people living in Gaza is in a difficult situation. The images below portray how people cook for their families over small fires near army tents, people living in poor shelter, displaced brothers in the heat. Currently, thousands of families are experiencing these problems and are trying to find shelter and are looking for a place to shelter. Access to clean water and basic food is daily lacking.

“With your support, we will continue delivering hot meals, clean water, and essential supplies.”

— Yedi Başak Field Team

Thanks to you, babies in Gaza receive nutrition

With the Baby Food Donation to Gaza campaign we have launched, we are providing the baby food necessary for the children of Gaza to grow up healthy. Thank you for your support.
