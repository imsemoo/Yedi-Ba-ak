Message

With your trust, we turn compassion into sustained impact

Dear friends; as our Lord has said, “The situation of those who spend their wealth in the way of Allah is like a grain that gives seven ears of ears, one hundred in each ear.” Established in 2016, Yedi Başak was born to embody the values that make human life noble.

Yedi Başak Humanitarian Aid Association is an international, independent NGO that aims to alleviate the pain and suffering of all needy people by providing material and spiritual support—regardless of religion, language, race, or geography. Through fast and accountable action, our projects have touched the lives of millions in three continents.

We serve with integrity and transparency. Every lira entrusted to us is treated as an amanah. We report, measure, and improve—so your kindness travels farther.

Our teams provide essentials like food, shelter, clean water, and healthcare, and we mobilize in emergencies with agility and care. We also invest in sustainable development—livelihoods, education, and permanent works—so families can stand on their own feet with dignity.

While you are benevolent and rain down like drops of mercy, we experience the happiness of raising your kindness to the victims and oppressed. We want to reunite the world with the reviving breath of goodness. We thank you for your trust and support to Yedi Başak Humanitarian Aid Association.

Mustafa BULUT
Chairman of the Board
