About Us

Who are we?

Yedi Başak Humanitarian Relief Organization is an independent, non-profit international NGO founded in 2016 to preserve dignity and improve lives. We work with donors, partners and volunteers to deliver sustainable aid in food, water, health, education and emergency response — regardless of origin or category.

Our Values & Principles

We believe that impactful change begins with unwavering values. Every step we take is guided by our deep commitment to integrity, justice, and responsibility.

  • 1. Transparency

    We are accountable to our stakeholders as we strive to do our work in the best way possible.

  • 2. Institutionalism

    We are guided by values of integrity, justice, and responsibility in everything we do.

  • 3. Justice

    We treat people fairly because they are human — regardless of religion, language, race, gender or geography.

  • 4. Impartiality

    We deliver aid with fairness and respect, embracing all without discrimination.

Our Goals

What we offer?

Yedi Başak carries out projects to help people in need within the scope of food security, livelihood, water and sanitation, education, shelter, nutrition and health programs. By putting humanitarian needs at the center, we provide in-kind and cash assistance at home and abroad; we support education and health, permanent works and sustainable development projects. With reliable partners on 3 continents, our aim is to provide aid to all victims and oppressed people — regardless of religion, language, race or gender.
